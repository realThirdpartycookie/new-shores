❓ How to play

Goal: Settle your island and guide your people from Pioneers to Settlers to Citizens — and, with colonial spice, to ⚜️ Merchants.

  • 🏠 Houses grow when their needs are met and pay taxes. Keep them inside a ⚖️ Marketplace radius and supply 🐟 food.
  • Production buildings need a 🛣 road connection to the Warehouse (or a Marketplace/Depot) to deliver goods.
  • Chains: 🐑 Wool → 🧵 Cloth  ·  ⛏ Iron → 🔨 Tools  ·  🌾 Grain → 🥖 Bread  ·  🥔 Potatoes → 🥃 Liquor  ·  🌶️ Spice (colonies only!).
  • Upgrades: a full house upgrades automatically once the next tier is unlocked, its services are covered and the upgrade good (cloth / liquor / spice) is in stock. Hover a house to see what's holding it back.
  • 🚢 Trade any time — but prices drift with your trading. Watch for the ⚓ merchant ship: while she is docked her special deals beat the market.
  • ⛵ Expeditions: outfit a ship (gold, food, wood, tools) and send it beyond the map. It may return with goods, treasure, sea charts — or exotic seeds that teach your home island a new crop.
  • 🏝 Expansion: other islands have different fertilities — and 🌶️ spice never grows at home. Build a ⛵ Kontor at a foreign waterline to found a colony; cargo ships connect it to your stores automatically.
  • 🏴‍☠️ Pirates raid prosperous towns — build 🗼 Watchtowers near the coast. 🔥 Fires and 🤒 plague strike too: a 🚒 Fire Station and chapels limit the damage.
  • 🌙 Day & night: each day lasts five minutes — windows light up after dusk, fireflies rise in the glades, and the music turns soft. Storms bring rain, lightning and grounded fishers.
  • 🏆 Achievements chronicle your finest moments — check the toolbar.
  • 🧠 The Advisor (bottom left) is a small neural network reading your economy. It suggests the next move — click Do it to follow, or flip 🤖 Auto and let it govern: it builds, routes roads and trades on its own.

Controls: Left-click to build/select · drag to pan (flick to glide) · wheel to zoom · right-click/Esc cancels · WASD/arrows pan · Space pauses · 1/2/3 speed · T trade · H help.

Status icons: ! no road · ! missing nature (trees/water/ore/grass) · ! missing input good · ! storage full.
